# Upgrading to PRO

Upgrading to PRO removes all the limits imposed in the free version.

## Upgrading to PRO gives you
- Unlimited notes
- Unlimited days of use
- iCloud sync

## How to Upgrade to Pro
1. Open app menu
2. Click on 'Upgrade to Pro' to open Purchase window
3. Click on buy. Confirm the price and approve the transaction

## FAQ
### Is Quick Note Pro a subscription?
NO. Quick Note Pro is a one-time purchase now and forever


## Note:
- You should have appropriate payment enabled in your apple account
- For parental permission, you purchase will reflect automatically after approval
